Weekly Update

On Monday, we worked remote. President Trump held a last minute rally for Kentucky’s Republicans on Monday night, and there were reports of multiple road closures. I really enjoyed working from home! The best part was probably my midday nap (something that unintentionally stretched to 2 and a half hours). This was nice, as I was refreshed and able to better absorb our Laracast videos. Thankfully, I was able to make up for this lost time by working well into the evening.

Tuesday was both election day and the start of the UK basketball season! Republicans swept the state in all offices except governor, where Andy Beshear beat incumbent Matt Bevin by roughly 5,000 votes. I had a lot of fun watching the results trickle in. When it was down to the wire, I pulled up historical voting data on the remaining 16 precincts and was trying to predict which way the needle would fall based on voter turnout in the 2015 election!

UK opened its season against #1 Michigan State. The Cats held on for a victory, and are excepted to move from a #2 to a #1 early next week! With each rotating batch of talent, I like to pick a few players to root for. So far I’m a big fan of Tyrese Maxey and Nate Sestina.

This week our Bootcamp learned that I write my alphabet in a weird manner. This is news to me. I had no idea how I wrote the letter “e” until asked to do so in front of the class. I still think that I am just ahead of the curve on this one — maybe one day everyone will catch up and starting writing their "e"’s from the bottom!

Finally, on Friday we had a happy hour for out Bootcamp cohort! We all met up at Marikka’s around 5:30. We had a pretty good turnout with over half of the class showing up! Lots of games were played. I’ve included a picture of Taylor and I losing a 2 against 1 Foosball match against Abram. I was really glad that so many of the cohort came out!
